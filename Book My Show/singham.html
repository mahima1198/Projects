PATNI SINGHAM PATI CHINGHAM ( Comedy , Drama ) 80♥

Synopsis


ARTISTS Arvind Vaidh, Nimesh Shah, Mallika Shah.

Patni Singham is a comedy play for one and all and is a perfect dose of laughter.Its about a family who is ordinary but due to a particular incident becomes extraordinary. Thus starts the saga of fun, laughter, emotions and a heart filled message. A perfect entertainer.
